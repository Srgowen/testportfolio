About Dr. Sarah Mitchell

Dedicated to providing personalized, evidence-based mental health care

Ph.D. Clinical Psychology
Licensed Psychologist
Board Certified

Compassionate. Professional. Effective.

With over a decade of experience in clinical psychology, I am committed to helping individuals navigate life's challenges and achieve lasting positive change. My approach combines evidence-based therapeutic techniques with genuine compassion and understanding.

I specialize in treating anxiety disorders, depression, trauma, and relationship issues. My goal is to create a safe, non-judgmental space where you can explore your thoughts and feelings, develop coping strategies, and work towards your personal goals.
